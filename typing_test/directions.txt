Welcome to Manny's Typing Test!

Use this typing test to see your wpm (words per minute) or to practice typing.

Before the typing test begins there will be a 3 second countdown. Once the typing test
begins you will be given a randomly generated paragraph that you must type. Once
the test ends you shown your results (your wpm, accuracy, and time spent typing).

In order to start the typing test, you must exit to the menu and press the s key.
In order to return to the menu (from the typing test) you must press the ESC (escape) key.

Press ESC to return to the menu.
